Nancy Jackson, County Clerk

Nancy Jackson

Lincoln County Clerk

I am Nancy Jackson, elected in 2023 after 29 years of service in the Lincoln County Clerk’s Office. Our team is committed to providing friendly, efficient service and preserving the rich history of Lincoln County for generations to come.

Lincoln County is one of Kentucky’s three original counties. Our clerk’s office maintains records from the late 1700s to present. Our rich history makes us aware of the need to preserve our records while being able to accommodate our customers both online and in the office with accurate and efficient data management. We also manage motor vehicle services, elections, hunting and fishing licenses, notary appointments, and more.

Our website offers an overview of these services and links to helpful resources. Follow our Facebook page for real-time updates. Whether you’re visiting in person, online, or calling - we’re here to help.
