About the Clerk's Office

Our Mission

The Lincoln County Clerk’s Office is dedicated to serving the citizens of Lincoln County with integrity, transparency, and efficiency. We preserve vital records, manage elections, and provide a wide range of public services.

Our History

Lincoln County was established on December 17, 1780 becoming one of the first counties of Kentucky. The county was formed out of Kentucky County, Virginia. Lincoln County Seat is Stanford and is borded by Boyle, Casey, Rockcastle, Garrard, Pulaski counties. Other Incorporated Towns include Stanford, Crab Orchard, Hustonville, McKinney

Meet the Clerk

Nancy Jackson and was elected in 2023 after serving 29 years in the Lincoln County Clerk’s Office. She has seen many changes in the office over the years and has enjoyed a career of serving the people of Lincoln County. She continues to strive to provide friendly and efficient service to everyone that comes in to the County Clerks offices.
